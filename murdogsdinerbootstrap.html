About Murdog's

Murdog’s has been a family owned restaurant in Caro, Michigan for the last 15 years. Murdog’s serves breakfast, lunch, dinner, and coffee, while offering take-out and dine-in for everyone’s convenience.

You can “Like” us on Facebook!, or call and place an order (989) 673-3805, or Find our location!
